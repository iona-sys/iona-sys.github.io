About Iona

Iona Systems is a Vancouver-based R&D Lab run by founder Peter Van Garderen.

Iona specializes in prototyping decentralized record-keeping applications and platforms.

Iona has incubated and spun off the Landano (land right records) project and Orcfax (oracle records) project.

Our current R&D is focused on moving the Arkly.io decentralized archiving service into production for use by other projects and platforms.
